Cox Mill High School

Cox Mill High School (CMHS) is a public, comprehensive high school located at 1355 Cox Mill Road, Concord, North Carolina. Opened in 2009, CMHS is part of the Cabarrus County Schools District and proudly serves students in grades 9-12 from the growing Concord and western Cabarrus communities.

A Cox Mill, our mission is to graduate all students as empowered problem solvers, continual learners, and productive citizens. Guided by this purpose, we strive to create a welcoming, collaborative environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Vision and Values

Cox Mill envisions a community of students, families, staff, and local partners who share high expectations for learning and achievement. We value:

  • A safe and motivating learning environment
  • Personalized instruction that meets the needs of all learners
  • Integrity, respect, and responsibility in all that we do
  • Strong family and community partnerships
  • A commitment to excellence and continuous improvement

Academics & Programs

CMHS offers a rigorous curriculum that combines core academics with modern learning experiences designed to prepare students for college, careers, and life beyond the classroom.

Our academic highlights include:

  • Consistently high student performance in math and reading (well above state averages)
  • A 96% graduation rate, among the best in North Carolina
  • Advanced Placement (AP) and Honors courses across multiple disciplines
  • The Academy of Information Technology (AoIT), offering specialized pathways in programming, multimedia design, and IT careers
  • Hands-on learning opportunities through internships and community partnerships

Campus & Facilities

Our modern 232,500-square-foot campus includes:

  • 36 classrooms and 10 science labs
  • Biotechnology and vocational labs
  • Art and music studios
  • A 650-seat auditorium
  • A full gymnasium, auxiliary gym, and athletic fields for football, baseball, softball, soccer, and tennis

These state-of-the-art spaces allow students to explore academics, athletics, and the arts in an environment built for success.

Hack Club

About

Hack Club is a global nonprofit organization that empowers high school students to learn, create, and solve real problems through technology. Built entirely by students, Hack Club provides an open, welcoming environment where young people can explore coding, pursue passion projects, and develop real-world skills. Whether you're building your first website or launching a full hackathon, Hack Club encourages creativity, experimentation, and learning by doing.

Mission

Hack Club's mission is to help every high school student become a creator. Hack Club exists to provide the tools, community, and support that enable teens to explore the world of technology through hands-on building. By giving students the freedom to pursue their own ideas and collaborate with peers, Hack Club helps foster genuine passion for learning and innovation.

Values

  • Curiosity

    Hack Club encourages students to explore, question, and discover. Curiosity drives creativity and growth.

  • Building Over Lectures

    The organization prioritizes hands-on creation. Students learn by making real projectsâ€“apps, websites, games, tools, and more.

  • Community

    Hack Club fosters a global network where students collaborate, share ideas, and help one another succeed.

  • Inclusivity

    Hack Club welcomes students of all backgrounds and experience levels. Anyone with an interest in building something is encourages to join.

  • Student-Led Leadership

    Clubs are operated by students (like ours), allowing members to lead initiatives, shape club direction, and contribute their own ideas.

What Hack Club Does

Hack Club provides students with opportunities to explore coding and technology in a supportive, student-driven environment. Activities across Hack Club chapters often include:

  • Developing websites, applications, and games
  • Participating in workshops and project-based learning
  • Contributing to open-source software
  • Exploring design, engineering, AI, and hardware
  • Hosting hackathons, build sessions, or collaborative events
  • Engaging with Hack Club's global programs and online community

Each meeting centers on building, experimenting, and learning alongside peers.

Why Students Join

Students choose to join Hack Club for the chance to become part of a creative and ambitious global community. Membership provides:

  • Real-world coding and technical skills
  • Project experience that strengthens portfolios and college applications
  • Access to a large network of supportive peers
  • Free tools, resources, and opportunities to learn
  • Exposure to web development, hardware, AI, design, and more
  • Leadership opportunities within club or event organization
  • A fun, collaborative environment focused on building

Hack Club offers students a place to explore their interests without academic pressureâ€”just creativity and community.

Our Story

Hack Club was founded on the belief that high school students can accomplish extraordinary things when given the right tools and space to create. Since its start, the organization has grown into an international movement of student-led clubs spanning numerous countries. Emphasizing open-source collaboration and peer-to-peer learning, Hack Club continues to expand by empowering students to lead and innovate.

Today, Hack Club members around the world build everything from websites and art tools to robotics projects, nonprofits, and major hackathons. The organization remains driven by the creativity and passion of students who are committed to learning, experimenting, and making an impact.
